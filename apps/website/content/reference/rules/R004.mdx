## What

Checks that the skill's `name` follows the supported naming format.

## Why

Agents and tooling use the name as a stable skill identifier. Invalid names can prevent discovery or create inconsistent references.

## How to fix

Change the frontmatter `name` to a valid, concise identifier. Use lowercase words separated by hyphens.
