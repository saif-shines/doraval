## What

Uses the configured language model to find instructions in the skill that conflict with each other.

## Why

Contradictory instructions force the agent to guess which rule wins. That makes the skill's behavior inconsistent across tasks and models.

## How to fix

Remove one of the conflicting instructions or state the precedence explicitly. Keep exceptions next to the rule they modify.
