| **What** | Reports (FYI) when advanced frontmatter fields beyond `name` / `description` are present. Default package: **strict** only. |
| **Why** | Extra fields can be useful, but they are agent-specific. This is informational so authors notice non-portable metadata. |

## How to fix

Confirm each advanced field is intentional and supported by your target agents. Remove unused keys. Prefer body instructions when a field is experimental.
