## What

Checks that the skill starts with a YAML frontmatter block delimited by `---` lines.

## Why

Frontmatter identifies the skill and describes when an agent should load it. Without that metadata, the skill cannot participate reliably in discovery.

## How to fix

Add a YAML frontmatter block at the start of `SKILL.md`. Include at least the required `name` and `description` fields.
