## What

LLM-tier judgment: instructions in the skill body should be unambiguous — one clear meaning per rule, no contradictory or foggy guidance.

## Why

Long, essay-like skills often hide conflicting advice. Ambiguity multiplies across millions of invocations; the agent cannot “read between the lines” reliably under load.

## How to fix

1. Split multi-topic skills or move side topics into `references/`.
2. Replace hedging (“maybe”, “consider”, “perhaps”) with a decision or a condition.
3. If two paths are valid, state the **decision rule** that picks between them.
4. Mechanical ambiguity cues also surface under [R019](/reference/rules/R019).
