## What

LLM-tier judgment: instructions should be executable — the agent can act without inventing missing steps or APIs.

## Why

Trivia and background do not ship work. Prefer directives and outcomes over encyclopedias. Over-specified micro-workflows also fail: they block recovery when reality diverges from the author’s three test prompts.

## How to fix

- Lead with **what success looks like**, then constraints.
- Give one concrete example ([R017](/reference/rules/R017)).
- Say why a constraint exists when it generalizes (“model Y is deprecated and returns errors”).
- Avoid overfitting to a single happy path; cover the failure you care about in `scenarios.yaml` ([R027](/reference/rules/R027)).
- Fragile multi-step order → script, not a novel-length procedure.
