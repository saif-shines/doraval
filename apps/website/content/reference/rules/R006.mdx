## What

Checks that `SKILL.md` contains Markdown instructions after its frontmatter block.

## Why

Metadata can trigger a skill, but the body tells the agent what to do. An empty body leaves the skill without executable guidance.

## How to fix

Add concrete instructions after the closing `---` line. Describe the workflow, required constraints, and expected output.
