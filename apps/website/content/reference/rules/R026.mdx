| **What** | LLM-tier: skill scope is focused — not a grab-bag of unrelated jobs. |
| **Why** | Wide-scope skills are hard to trigger correctly and hard to maintain. Progressive disclosure prefers one job per skill. |

## How to fix

Split into separate skills or move secondary topics into `references/` loaded only when needed. Tighten the description to the primary job ([R025](/reference/rules/R025)).
